\subsection{Failure Pattern Analysis}

The protocol-defined failure-pattern differentiation reveals five ordered error categories. These are protocol-defined constructs, not hidden causal mechanisms. The ordinal structure enables relative comparison within the protocol but does not support cardinal interpretation.
