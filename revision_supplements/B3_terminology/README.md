## Key Content
- **9 allowed terms** with required conditions and example sentences
- **10 forbidden terms** with replacements and risk types
- **5 external agreement wording rules** (3 kappa tiers)
- **5 ordinal boundary wording rules** (calibration, R2, residual, entropy, onset)
- **Response letter phrase bank** for common reviewer concerns
